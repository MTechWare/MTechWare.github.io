MVault

A secure, offline password manager built with React, TypeScript, and Tauri. Store logins, cards, API keys, notes, bookmarks, and identities — all locally on your device.

Download for Windows Explore Features

Why MVault

Privacy First, Always

No cloud. No servers. No subscriptions. Your data never leaves your machine.

🔐

100% Offline

All data stored locally via browser localStorage. No internet required.

🛡️

PIN Protected

Minimum 8-character PIN policy with automatic lockout after repeated failures.

💾

Fully Portable

Single .exe — drop it on a USB drive, Desktop, or network share and go.

Capabilities

Vault Features

Everything you need to manage your digital identity, organized and secure.

🔑

Logins & Credentials

Store usernames, passwords, URLs, and notes for every account. Copy credentials in one click.

💳

Cards & Payment Info

Securely save credit cards, debit cards, and payment details for quick access when you need them.

🔗

API Keys

Keep your API keys, tokens, and secrets organized and easily accessible for development work.

📝

Secure Notes

Store sensitive text — license keys, recovery codes, private notes — in an encrypted-at-rest vault.

🔖

Bookmarks

Save and organize URLs with import support for Chrome and Edge HTML bookmark exports.

👤

Identities

Store personal info like names, addresses, phone numbers, and emails for quick form-filling.

📁

Folder Organization

Group items into folders with full drag-and-drop support. Keep your vault tidy and navigable.

⚙️

Password Generator

Generate strong, random passwords with a built-in strength checker to audit your security.

🌐

Browser Extension

Auto-extracted extension for Chrome, Edge, and Brave. Autofill credentials directly from your vault.
